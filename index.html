02 — First Term

Prelim Showcase

Term Reflection

↓

This term served as an introduction to the key concepts and lessons covered in the course “Advanced Database.” During this period, our quizzes and activities focused on strengthening our understanding of the fundamental principles of databases. We reviewed and recalled the essential SQL syntax and database operations that we previously learned in our first database course, such as creating tables, managing records, and writing queries.

In addition, the term helped us refresh our knowledge of database structures, relationships, and management techniques, which are important foundations for more advanced topics. Through continuous practice and assessments, we were able to improve our familiarity with database commands and develop a deeper understanding of how databases are designed and maintained in real-world systems.

03 — Second Term

Midterm Showcase

Term Reflection

↓

All the lessons this term helped us improve our knowledge of databases, especially in using commands correctly and understanding when to apply them. Through our activities and discussions, we gained a better understanding of database management and developed skills that will help us in real-world applications.

The lessons also gave us an overview of the topics that may appear in our database certification exam. With enough knowledge and practice, we can become more prepared and confident in passing the certification exam during the finals.

04 — Final Term

Finals Showcase

Term Reflection

↓

All the lessons we had this term helped us gain a deeper understanding of databases and their importance in managing and organizing data efficiently. Through discussions, hands-on activities, and laboratory exercises, we learned how to properly use database concepts, commands, and tools that are essential in real-world applications. These lessons strengthened our knowledge and improved our confidence in working with database systems.

In addition, the term provided us with a clear overview of the topics and skills that would appear in the certification exam. The review sessions, quizzes, and mock examinations helped us practice and evaluate our understanding of the lessons discussed. These activities prepared us mentally and academically, allowing us to identify our strengths and improve the areas where we needed more practice. Because of this preparation, we became more confident and capable of successfully passing the certification exam.
